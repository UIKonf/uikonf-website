mytaxi Stage at UIKonf 2019

mytaxi Stage on a Boat

This year we have bunch of new things at UIKonf. One of them is the parallel stage hosted by our Platinum Sponsor, mytaxi.

This stage will be on a boat anchored in front of our venue, RadialSystem, and will feature a workshop and two talks held by the mytaxi team.
