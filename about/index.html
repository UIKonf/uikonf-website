About UIKonf

UIKonf is an independent conference for serious iOS developers that is going to be organized again in Berlin in 2015 by:

  • Engin Kurutepe

    Used to be a Ph.D. student. Bought an iPhone when the first iPhone SDK was released and has been building iOS apps ever since. Did not get around to finishing his thesis. Organizes CocoaHeads Berlin. Glider pilot in training.

  • Maxim Zaks

    Software developer with a history in IDE development, Web development and even Enterprise Java development (He was young and under bad influence). Nowadays working as a game developer (preferably iOS). Regular visitor and occasional speaker at conferences.

  • Sabine Geithner

    Biotechnologist by training who started developing apps about a year ago. Has been active in the developer community since June 2012, co-organizing workshops for Rails Girls Berlin.

  • Diana Arce

    Artist, researcher and trickster with an eye for detail and a love of creating spaces and events for new experiences, active engagement and knowledge exchange. She is the founder of Founder of Politaoke and ArtistsWAC and develops projects and events for all kinds of organizations internationally.

Previous Organizers

UIKonf was originally organized by Chris Eidhof, Matt Patterson, Peter Bihr and Max Krüger. Due to personal circumstances they transferred UIKonf in October 2014 to the current team.

  • Chris Eidhof

    http://eidhof.nl

    An iOS and Mac developer who’s been building iOS apps since the first public release of the SDK. He created the magazine objc.io and organized Alt Tech Talks Berlin. Recently, he has been building a new Mac app for creating beautiful presentations: Deckset.

  • Matt Patterson

    http://werkstatt.io

    A long-time developer of web apps. He’s a regular speaker at conferences and user groups. He organised the first History Hackday and was one of the organizers of NoSQL Europe 2010. He also helps mentor the Ruby Monsters Rails Girls Berlin study group.

  • Peter Bihr

    http://thewavingcat.com

    Gets up in the morning to connect people and ideas. A digital strategy guy by day, at night he organizes independent community events like UIKonf, Things, Cognitive Cities Conference, Ignite Berlin or TEDxKreuzberg. He also serves as Program Director at NEXT Berlin.

  • Max Krüger

    http://maxkrueger.com

    Interested in spaces, experiences and technologies to foster collaboration, creativity and the active exchange of knowledge. He worked on several highly regarded projects including ThIngsCon, the Global Innovation Lounge and the Creative Space Explorer.
