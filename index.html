UIKonf 2015 - Berlin, May 17-20

We are sold out!

UIKonf 2015 will take place at Heimathafen Neukölln, Berlin. In addition to covering the hottest topics in app development, UIKonf 2015 will also be a great place to meet and connect with great people.

Make sure to get the app if you are attending.

Sunday, 17th May 2015

Social activities in the afternoon (starting around 15:00) – get to know some nice people before the conference starts. If you are part of UIKonf 2015, you should have received an email with details about the tours. Please get in touch if you did not receive our event information.

  • A visit to the Berlin Museum of Technology
  • A guided tour of a World War 2 bomb shelter
  • Bike Tour with Ole Begemann
  • A guided tour of the Tempelhof Airport
  • A boat tour on the river Spree
  • Berlin Music Tour

A kick-off in the evening at InFarm from 19:00 on – have a cold beer, chat with other attendees and pick up your conference badge.

Monday & Tuesday - Reactive Native Workshop by Facebook

Location: Heimathafen Neukölln, smaller room upstairs
Time: 11:30 am - 1:00 pm

We are happy to also host a workshop by Facebook engineer Nick Lockwood. The workshop will be an introduction to React Native from a native iOS developer's perspective. Nick will cover:

  • What React Native is, and why you would use it
  • Building and debugging a React Native application
  • Integrating React Native views into an existing app
  • Creating native iOS modules and views for use in a React application

The workshop will be repeated on each day and include a presentation and live coding demonstration. The workshop is free to attend, be sure to be on time to get a seat.
