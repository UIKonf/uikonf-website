About UIKonf

UIKonf is Berlin's independent conference for serious iOS developers. Taking place from May 22nd - 25th 2016 in the cosy atmosphere of Heimathafen Neuk√∂lln it is a great place to meet and connect with experienced developers from all over Europe.

UIKonf features inspiring speakers giving talks on cutting edge topics in iOS development, mobile design and business.

But UIKonf is not only about gathering knowledge. We want to facilitate new connections and friends amongst our great community by providing an inclusive setting where all participants feel safe and welcome. In order to achieve that, we expect all to abide by our Code of Conduct.

To help people connect, we framed UIKonf with social events which provide an informal environment for meeting like-minded people.

Call for Proposals

Contribute to UIKonf 2016 by proposing and / or selecting talks through our vestibule. Talks should be no longer than 30 minutes plus discussion time. Please do not include a bio or link to your personal profiles. We want to keep the process as anonymous as possible. The rules in short:
  • Please submit only one proposal per person.
  • Talks should be no longer than 30 minutes plus discussion time.
  • Please do not include a bio or link to your personal profiles. We want to keep the process as anonymous as possible. We do understand though that the topic of your talk might give away some hints of who you are. There is no need to go to ridiculous lengths to conceal your identity if it makes the proposal more difficult to write

The proposal process consists of three steps:

Phase 1 - Submission

Deadline: February 15th, 10am CET

You submit your proposal until 15th February. Others will help you refine and focus it by making suggestions. You keep updating your proposal bearing those suggestions in mind.

Phase 2 - Pre-Selection

Deadline: February 21st

From February 15th - 21st we - the organizer team - will select the best 30 candidates. The aim of this step is to ensure a diversity of topics. You increase your chances to be selected by writing a compelling proposal. Check these sample proposals for inspiration.

Phase 3 - Final Voting

Deadline: February 29th, 10am CET

Until February 29th everyone can vote on the preselected proposals and decide which ones are to be presented at UIKonf 2016.
