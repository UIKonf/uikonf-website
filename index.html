UIKonf 2015 - Berlin, May 17-20

Take part in Berlin's independent conference for serious iOS developers. UIKonf 2015 will take place at Heimathafen Neukölln, Berlin. In addition to covering the hottest topics in app development, UIKonf 2015 will also be a great place to meet and connect with great people.

Here is the schedule (work in progress):

Sunday, May 17th

Social activities in the afternoon (starting around 14:00) – get to know some nice people before the conference starts.

  • A visit to the Berlin Museum of Technology
  • A guided tour of a World War 2 bomb shelter
  • Bike Tour with Ole Begemann
  • A guided tour of the Tempelhof Airport
  • A boat tour on the river Spree
  • Berlin Music Tour

A kick-off in the evening from 19:00 on – have a cold beer, chat with other attendees and pick up your conference badge.

Monday & Tuesday, May 18th-19th

Conference at Heimathafen Neukölln – 18 confirmed speakers, full schedule coming soon

Wednesday, May 20th

Hackathon (from ~09:00 to ~19:00) – Hack on fresh new ideas with cool people.

Tickets

If you prefer to book directly through ti.to, go to https://ti.to/uikonf/2015

Fees exclude VAT, but include free coffee, drinks and lunch during the conference, social activities on Sunday afternoon and lots of new knowledge to take home. To help us planning the social events, please select your preferred activity when booking your ticket. You can always edit it later.

A note for corporate buyers

If you are a corporate customer and would like to send a larger team (5+ people) over, please get in touch. We can arrange a bulk discount and work with your procurement to make the purchase as seamless as possible.

Free Scholarship Tickets

If you are unable to afford a ticket, you can apply for a free scholarship ticket until February 28th, 2015. These tickets are awarded to talented students and iOS newcomers.

Call For Proposals

Our Call for Proposals is finished.

We know you can't wait to find out about the selected talks but we won't reveal them all at once. We are already contacting the authors of the selected talks and we will be announcing two speakers every week for the coming weeks. Also, if you don't receive an email from us about your proposal in the next few days, unfortunately that means your proposal has not been selected.

Information for Sponsors

UIKonf is a great place to meet talented and experienced developers in a less formal context. If you are interested in supporting UIKonf, get in touch with Engin Kurutepe by sending an email to questions@uikonf.com.
